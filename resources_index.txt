## 💡 How to Use These Resources

### Individual Practice
1. **Start with the Starter Pack** to get oriented
2. **Work through templates** alongside video lessons
3. **Use quality checklists** to self-assess your work
4. **Reference case studies** to see real applications

### Team Training
1. **Download the Complete Toolkit** for team distribution
2. **Use case studies** as discussion materials
3. **Assign different templates** to team members for practice
4. **Schedule review sessions** using quality checklists

### Client Work (Consultants)
1. **Customize templates** with your branding and approaches
2. **Use case studies** to demonstrate methodology value
3. **Reference quick guides** during client conversations
4. **Share lesson links** to build client capacity
